Detailed Analysis:
   • System correctly identifies ~57% of individual characters
   • Full plate matching is challenging (only ~19% correct)
   • Length prediction is slightly short (predicts 8.6 vs actual 9.6)
   • Confidence scores generally low (<0.4), indicating OCR uncertainty

Known Challenges:
   ✗ Indian plate format variability (2-letter state + 2 digits + 1-2 letters + 4 digits)
   ✗ Printed text varies in quality across vehicles
   ✗ Pre-trained models not specifically tuned for Indian plates
   ✗ Lighting, angle, dirt can affect readability

Recommendations for Improvement:
   1. Fine-tune EasyOCR on Indian plate-specific dataset
   2. Use ensemble voting (EasyOCR + Tesseract + PaddleOCR)
   3. Implement format-aware HMM/CRF postprocessing
   4. Apply advanced preprocessing (contrast enhancement, deskew, denoise)
   5. Generate synthetic training data for OCR fine-tuning
   6. Implement confidence filtering with fallback strategies
